About Aria

Aria Monroe is an award-winning actress, singer, and humanitarian known for her powerful voice and mesmerizing performances on screen and stage.

Her career began at the age of 14 with a breakout role in a Broadway production. Since then, she has starred in over 20 films and released three critically acclaimed albums.

Outside her career, Aria advocates for women's rights and global education for children. She's been recognized by several international organizations for her philanthropy.
